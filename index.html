About Us

Dr. M. Alex Pandian, BSMS, MD(S)

Founder & Consultant — GetWell Herbal Care

GetWell Herbal Care is a Siddha-based wellness clinic founded by Dr. M. Alex Pandian, a seasoned expert in gastrointestinal and liver health. With over 8 years of clinical experience, he integrates traditional herbal wisdom with modern lifestyle-based care.

His core expertise lies in managing chronic conditions such as GERD, IBS, IBD, fatty liver, and pancreatic issues through safe, non-invasive methods. He emphasizes preventive care, patient education, and natural healing.

At GetWell Herbal Care, we are committed to delivering personalized Siddha solutions, rooted in ancient Tamil medical knowledge and backed by modern clinical experience.
