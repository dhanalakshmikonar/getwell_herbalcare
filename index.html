About Us

GetWell Herbal Care is a Siddha-based wellness clinic founded by Dr. M. Alex Pandian, a seasoned expert in gastrointestinal and liver health. With over 8 years of clinical experience, he integrates traditional herbal wisdom with modern lifestyle-based care.
